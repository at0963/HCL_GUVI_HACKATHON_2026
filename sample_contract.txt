AND

CLIENT: ABC Enterprises, a partnership firm registered in Mumbai, Maharashtra, having its principal place of business at 456 Commercial Street, Mumbai, Maharashtra 400001 (hereinafter referred to as the "Client")

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

1.1 The Service Provider shall provide the following services to the Client:

1.2 The Service Provider shall use reasonable efforts to perform the services in a professional and timely manner.

2. DELIVERABLES

2.1 The Service Provider shall deliver the following:

2.2 All deliverables are subject to Client's approval and acceptance within 7 business days of delivery.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 Total project fee is INR 15,00,000 (Fifteen Lakh Rupees only).

3.2 Payment schedule:

    - 30% upon 50% project completion: INR 4,50,000

3.3 All payments shall be made via bank transfer within 15 days of invoice.

3.4 Late payments shall attract interest at 18% per annum.

3.5 Service Provider may suspend services if payment is delayed by more than 30 days.

4. PROJECT TIMELINE

4.1 Project commencement date: February 1, 2026
4.2 Expected completion date: July 31, 2026
4.3 Timeline extensions require written approval from both parties.

5.1 All intellectual property created specifically for this project shall be owned exclusively by the Client upon full payment.

5.2 Service Provider retains rights to pre-existing intellectual property, general methodologies, and frameworks.

6.2 Confidential information includes but is not limited to business plans, technical specifications, client data, and financial information.

6.3 Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of 3 years.

6.4 Breach of confidentiality may result in immediate termination and legal action.

8.1 Service Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client.

8.2 IN NO EVENT SHALL SERVICE PROVIDER BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, CONSEQUENTIAL, INCIDENTAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

8.3 Service Provider shall not be liable for delays or failures due to circumstances beyond reasonable control.

9. INDEMNIFICATION

9.1 Client agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Service Provider from any claims arising from Client's use of the deliverables or breach of this Agreement.

9.2 Service Provider agrees to indemnify Client against claims of intellectual property infringement arising from the deliverables.

10.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

10.2 Client may terminate immediately upon material breach by Service Provider if not cured within 15 days of written notice.

10.3 Service Provider may terminate immediately if Client fails to make payment for more than 45 days.

11.1 Any changes to scope of work must be requested in writing.

11.2 Service Provider shall provide cost and timeline estimates for changes within 5 business days.

11.3 Changes shall be implemented only upon written approval and payment terms agreement.

12. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

12.1 Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through good faith negotiations.

12.2 If negotiations fail, disputes shall be resolved through arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996.

12.3 Arbitration shall be conducted in Bangalore, Karnataka, with one arbitrator mutually agreed upon.

12.4 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on both parties.

13. FORCE MAJEURE

13.1 Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to events beyond reasonable control including natural disasters, acts of government, riots, strikes, or pandemics.

13.2 Affected party must notify the other party within 7 days of force majeure event.

13.3 If force majeure continues for more than 60 days, either party may terminate without penalty.

14. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

14.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

14.2 Subject to arbitration clause, courts at Bangalore, Karnataka shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

15. GENERAL PROVISIONS

15.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements.

15.2 No modification of this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties.

15.3 If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.

15.4 Neither party may assign this Agreement without prior written consent of the other party.

15.5 Notices under this Agreement shall be sent via registered post or email to addresses specified herein.

15.6 This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
